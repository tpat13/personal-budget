What is a Personal Budget?

A budget is a way of tracking money coming in, money going out, and a way to set smart financial goals. This can help you see if you were saving money or if you were spending more than you earn. A budget is a great reality check and might be painful at times.

Why Budget?

A personal budget can help you understand where your money is going. Budgeting makes you aware of your spending habits which helps shape your overall financial goals.
